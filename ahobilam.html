Hyderabad → Ahobilam → Gandikota → Hyderabad

Your 3-Day Pilgrimage & Adventure

This interactive guide plans your journey from the hustle of Hyderabad to the serene Nallamala forests and the grandeur of the Erramala hills. You will cover approximately 850 km, trek through the holy hills of Ahobilam, witness the crystal pools of Mahanandi, marvel at the Yaganti idol, and watch the sunrise over the Grand Canyon of India at Gandikota.

Dinner at Kurnool

Stop at food courts near Kurnool. From here, divert towards Nandyal/Allagadda.

Reach Ahobilam / Allagadda

Check into Haritha Resort (Ahobilam) or a hotel in Allagadda (better amenities). Rest for the trek tomorrow.

View Route Map (Ahobilam → Mahanandi)
06:00

Lower Ahobilam

Visit the main Prahlada Varada Swamy temple first. It's usually less crowded early in the morning.

08:00 TREK

Upper Ahobilam Trek

Drive to Upper Ahobilam. Visit Ahobila Narasimha (Main Upper Temple). From there, trek to Kroda (Varaha) and Jwala Narasimha (Spot of anger). The path to Jwala is scenic but steep.

Note: Visit Ugra Stambham only if you are fit; it's a difficult vertical climb.
13:00

Lunch & Rest

Simple vegetarian meals available at the Arya Vysya Anna Satram or local canteens.

15:00

Pavana & Bhargava

Hire a local jeep to visit Pavana Narasimha (deep forest, bumpy ride). On return, visit Bhargava Narasimha near a small lake.

19:00 DECIDE

Night Stay Choices

Option A Nandyal City

Drive ~60km from Ahobilam.

  • Pro: Better hotels (AC/Hot water), good restaurants.
  • Con: 30 min drive to Mahanandi temple in morning.
Option B Mahanandi Temple

Drive ~75km from Ahobilam.

  • Pro: Stay at Haritha cottages right at temple. Early morning dip is magical.
  • Con: Very basic amenities, limited food options.
View Route Map (Mahanandi → Gandikota)
07:00

Mahanandi Temple

Drive to Mahanandi (from Nandyal it's very close). Swim in the Pushkarini (temple tank) where water is crystal clear and warm irrespective of season. Darshan of Lord Shiva.

11:00

Yaganti Uma Maheshwara

Drive to Yaganti (approx 60km). Famous for the Growing Nandi. Explore the caves (Agastya Cave, Venkateswara Cave) by climbing the steep steps.

14:00

Banaganapalle

Quick stop at Banaganapalle fort or Nawab's bungalow if interested. Have lunch here.

17:00 VIEW

Drive to Gandikota

Drive to Gandikota (approx 1.5 - 2 hrs). If you reach by sunset, head straight to the Penna river gorge view point.

20:00 CAMP

Stay at Gandikota

Stay at Haritha Resort (book in advance) or try tent camping near the gorge organized by locals. Enjoy the starry night.

View Route Map (Return via Alampur)
06:00

Gandikota Sunrise

Watch the sun rise over the canyon. It is the best time for photography. Explore the Fort, Granary, and Ranganatha Swamy Temple ruins.

11:00

Belum Caves

Drive to Belum Caves (approx 60km). These are the second longest caves in India. Explore the underground passages, Meditation Hall, and Patalaganga.

Tip: It gets humid inside, carry water.
14:00 DRIVE

Drive to Alampur

Depart Belum. Drive towards Kurnool to reach Alampur (approx 140km / 3 hrs). Have lunch en route.

17:00 VISIT

Alampur Jogulamba

Visit the Jogulamba Temple (Shakti Peetham) and the 7th-century Navabrahma Temples. The architecture here is Chalukyan and unique.

21:30 HOME

Reach Hyderabad

Drive back to Hyderabad via NH44 (approx 215km). Reach home by night.

Explore the Destinations

Ahobilam

Pilgrimage + Trek

The only place where all 9 forms of Narasimha are worshipped. Split into Upper (Eguva) and Lower (Diguva) Ahobilam.

Must See: Jwala Narasimha, Ugra Stambham.
Vibe: Forest, Divine, Strenuous.

Mahanandi

Relaxation

Ancient Shiva temple famous for its fresh water pools. The water source is unknown and remains constant.

Must See: The main tank (Pushkarini).
Vibe: Peaceful, Lush Greenery.

Yaganti

History

Famous for the Nandi idol that locals believe is growing in size. Surrounded by magnificent caves.

Must See: Agastya Cave, Pushkarini.
Vibe: Rocky, Mystical.

Gandikota

Nature

Known as the Grand Canyon of India. A massive gorge formed by river Pennar cutting through Erramala hills.

Must See: Sunset/Sunrise Point, Fort Ruins.
Vibe: Epic, Windy, Photogenic.

Alampur

Heritage

Meeting point of Tungabhadra and Krishna rivers. Home to the 5th Shakti Peetham.

Must See: Jogulamba Temple, Navabrahma Temples.
Vibe: Ancient, Spiritual.
